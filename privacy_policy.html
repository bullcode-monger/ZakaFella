Privacy policy

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how the Savings & Loan Management System collects, uses, and protects user information.
We are committed to protecting member data and ensuring responsible data handling practices.

Information We Collect

The Platform may collect the following information:

  • Personal Information
    • Full name
    • Email address
    • Account role
  • Account Information
    • Encrypted password
    • Login session data
    • Membership duration
  • Financial Information
    • Savings transaction records
    • Loan applications
    • Loan approvals
    • Loan repayment records

How We Use Information

Information is used to:

  • Manage savings and loan records
  • Determine loan eligibility
  • Enforce role-based access control
  • Generate internal reports
  • Maintain system security and auditing

We do not use data for marketing purposes.

Data Security Measures

The Platform implements the following security measures:

  • Role-based access control
  • Server-side validation of financial calculations
  • Restricted database access

Data Storage

All information is stored in a secure MySQL database.
Access to financial and personal data is restricted to authorised roles only.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share member information with third parties.
Information may only be disclosed:

  • When required by law
  • For internal administrative or auditing purposes

Data Retention

Member data is retained:

  • For the duration of active membership
  • For auditing and financial record-keeping purposes
  • Users may request account deactivation subject to organisational policies.

User Rights

Users may:

  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request account deactivation
  • Raise concerns regarding data handling practices

Compliance

This Platform aims to align with the principles of the
Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA), ensuring lawful and responsible processing of personal information.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Continued use of the Platform constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
