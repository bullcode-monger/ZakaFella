How savings work in a Stokvel

A stokvel is built on a regular savings contributions form members. These contributions form the foundation of the group's financial strength.

  1. Regular contributions:
    Each member contributes a fixed amount weekly or monthly. The amount and schedule are agreed upon in the groups rules
  2. Building the shared fund:
    all contribution are combined into one communal fund This is the stokvel's total savings pool.
  3. Growing the fund:
    The savings can grow through:
    • Interest earned from member loans
    • New member contributions
    • Any addititional agreed income activities
    All profit go back into the shared fund for the benefit of the members
  4. Payout option:
    Depanding on the group structure, savings be:
    • Paid out to members at the end of the cycle
    • Paid out to members if they can nolonger pay til the end of the cycle
  5. Transparency:
    Members can view contribution, loan activity, and fund growth to ensure accountability and trust within the group.
