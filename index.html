Empowering savings together



Our ZakaFella is a community-driven platform created to help individuals save money together in a secure, transparent and convenient way. We combine the trusted tradition of group saving with modern digital technology to make managing contributions, tracking savings and distributing future simple and reliable.



Our goal is to empower memebers to achieve their financial goals through discipline, trust and collaboration. Whether members are saving for groceries, school expenses, emergencies or future investments, our platform provides an organized and safe environment where communities can grow financially together.



We believe that financial success is easier when people support each other. By using our platform, members gain access to structured savings plans, real-time contribution tracking, and improved communication within their stokvel groups.



Our mission is to preserve the strong cultural value of stokvel savings while enhancing it with efficiency, accountability and accessibility for the modern world.
