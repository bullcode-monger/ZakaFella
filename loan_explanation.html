We oparate as a peer to peer community based system

Our stokvel is built on community trust and shared financial growth. Members contribute regularly to a collective fund, and that fund is used to provide loans to members when needed. unlike banks, loans come directly from member contributions meaning members support each other.

How a Stokvel lean works?

  1. Member contributions:
    each member contributes fixed amount weekly or monthly into a communal fund
  2. Loan request:
    when a member needs financial assistance, they apply for a loan from the available funds
  3. Approval process:
    loans are approves by admin according to the group's rules and available funds
  4. Interest and growth:
    Loans include an agreed interest rate set by the group. The interest goes back into a share fund, helping it grow for the benefit of all members
  5. Repayment:
    Members repay their loans within the agreed timeframe. Responsible repayment ensures the system remains sustainable and fair to everyone
  6. Capped amount:
    The amount that a member can loan is capped at the amount that the member has saved + 30% of it.

Key principles

  • Loans are funded by members not external lenders
  • All rules and terms are defined in the group's constitution
  • interest earned benefit the entire group
  • The system is built on trust, transparency and accountability
